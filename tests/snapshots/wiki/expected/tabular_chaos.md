Appearance move to sidebar hide           From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia   Cycling race  The **2019 Tour of Romania** was a five-day cycling stage race that took place in Romania in September 2019. The race was the 52nd edition of the Tour of Romania. The tour was rated as a 2.1 event, as part of the 2019 UCI Europe Tour.
